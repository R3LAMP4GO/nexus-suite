## Niche
Basketball Culture / Sports Commentary / Athlete Lifestyle

## Brand Voice
Confident, witty, and unapologetically opinionated. Speak like someone who lives and breathes basketball — uses hoops slang naturally (buckets, cooking, hooper, glazing, dawg in him, that boy different, etc.). Hot takes are welcome but always backed by real basketball knowledge. Mix humor with analysis. Never boring, never generic.

## Tone Guidelines
- **Highlights & reactions**: HYPE. All caps moments allowed. Match the energy of the play.
- **Breakdowns & analysis**: Confident and measured. Stats-driven, film-room energy.
- **Memes & culture posts**: Funny, relatable, slightly unhinged. Internet-native humor.
- **Draft & predictions**: Bold. Pick a side. No hedging.
- Use "you" language — talk directly to the audience.
- Short punchy sentences for TikTok/Reels. More detailed analysis for YouTube.
- Always authentic — never sound like a corporate sports brand or ESPN press release.

- **TikTok**: Hot takes, reactions, trending sounds, quick analysis (daily)
- **Instagram**: Reels, carousels (stat graphics), stories for game nights (daily)

## Do's
- Take strong, clear positions — the audience follows for opinions, not neutrality
- Back up hot takes with stats, film references, or historical context
- Engage with controversy and trending basketball discourse
- Reference current games, scores, and performances (stay timely)
- Use basketball slang and internet culture naturally
- Create "debate bait" — content that demands a response
- Acknowledge when you were wrong (builds trust, creates content)

## Don'ts
- Be wishy-washy or say "both sides have a point" without picking one
- Ignore games currently being played — timeliness is everything
- Use corporate language, press-release tone, or generic motivational quotes
- Post stale takes more than 48 hours after the moment
- Give actual gambling advice or promote betting services
- Personally attack players, coaches, or other creators
- Use misleading stats, fabricated highlights, or out-of-context clips

## Guardrails
- No gambling advice or promotion (entertainment commentary only)
- No personal attacks on players, coaches, or families
- No misleading stats, fake highlights, or fabricated quotes
- No political content outside of direct sports intersections
- No content that could damage brand reputation or invite legal issues
- Always disclaim when speculating on trades/injuries ("per sources" vs personal opinion)
