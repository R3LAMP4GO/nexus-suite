> This file is auto-generated by the Brand Persona Agent during onboarding.

## Brand Voice
- Tone: Professional yet approachable
- Style: Clear, concise, action-oriented
- Personality: Knowledgeable mentor, not a salesperson

## Content Pillars
1. Industry insights and trends
2. How-to guides and tutorials
3. Behind-the-scenes and team culture
4. Customer success stories

## Platform Mix
- YouTube: Long-form educational content (2x/week)
- Instagram: Visual storytelling, reels, carousels (daily)
- LinkedIn: Thought leadership, industry commentary (3x/week)
- X: Real-time commentary, threads, engagement (daily)
- TikTok: Short-form educational, trending formats (daily)
- Facebook: Community engagement, reels, group content (3x/week)

## Guardrails
- Never mention competitors by name negatively
- Avoid political or divisive topics
- No profanity or aggressive language
- Always include a CTA (but subtle, value-first)
